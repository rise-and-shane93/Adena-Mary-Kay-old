About Me!

My passion is to be a Fearless Role Model and Beauty Risk Taker!

I started my Mary Kay business in March 1998 while on a “long term” hiatus from my high pressure government job. For my husband John and I, it was important that I stayed at home with my two sons, Seth and Shane. I realized the importance of being the one to teach them how to read, which they did by the age of three. Despite having the college diplomas on the wall, I knew she was doing exactly what I loved doing.

However, I also discovered that I wanted to be known as “Adena” and not simply “John’s wife” or “Seth and Shane’s mom.” When someone shared the Mary Kay opportunity with me, she felt that this Career Opportunity would benefit not just me and my family, but the community as well. I began my career in Charleston, South Carolina. The next year we relocated to Georgia which was a new adventure for everyone to experience. Seven moves in the next nine years kept us all very busy, but I knew that my Mary Kay business was always a priority.

Having lived in Northwest Georgia for the past eleven years, my family and I are still close and enjoy spending time together. My mother, Velva, now lives with us at Serendipity in Adairsville, GA.

It will be an experience you will truly enjoy.
